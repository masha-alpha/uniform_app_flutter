    Delivery

    • UK delivery is FREE for all orders.
    • Products marked will be delivered the next working day if ordered before midday on a working day, otherwise the following working day.
    • Products marked will be delivered in 2-4 working days.
    • Products marked will be delivered in 5-12 working days.
    • If you order products with different delivery times they will normally be sent in separate parcels.
    • Deliveries to Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands, Channel Islands and BFPO addresses may take an extra 1 or 2 working days.
    • Please allow 2 or 3 extra working days for tailored items.
    • "Working days" are Monday to Friday excluding public holidays.
    • All parcels are sent by Royal Mail, APC or DPD.
    • Payment methods accepted are credit and debit cards, cheques and postal orders.
    • Please see the Ordering, Payment and Delivery page for full details.
